Introduction

With an abundance of premium content, HBO Max holds great appeal for viewers. However, within the competitive streaming landscape, offering an exceptional user experience is crucial for subscriber retention and growth. This UX case study will uncover opportunities to optimize core elements of the HBO Max platform to better serve user needs.

Goal

The goal was to comprehensively test the mobile app's user experience. The interviews with users, ensured representation from both those familiar and unfamiliar with the HBO Max platform.

Some of the questions asked and tasks for the users while taking a surey were as follows:
1. Finding recent purchases.
2. Using the search function to find something to watch by genre.
3. Adding a title to their list.
4. Navigating to “My List”.
5. Playing a TV show from the “Continue Watching” list.
6. Navigating to the previous episode.
7. Navigating to the next episode.

Navigation

After the user testing, many issues were highlighted in the current app and the most prominent one was user navigation throughout the app

Content Discovery

Enhanced Search Filters: Introduce more refined search filters that allow users to explore content based on specific sub-genres, moods, themes, or other criteria beyond just titles.

Dynamic Recommendations: Utilize algorithms to provide personalized recommendations based on a user's viewing history, preferences, and behavior. This could help users discover new content tailored to their tastes.

Loading Transparency: Provide users with information about what content is loading next, similar to Netflix's "Lazy Loaded Titles," to reduce uncertainty and improve the overall experience.

The users who didn’t subscribe to HBO Max have these tags over 90 % of the covers. It gets difficult for the user to select which one he wants to see and which of the content is free.

Competitve Analysis

Other straming apps have personalized recommendations, and easily accessible search and browse functionalities, enhancing user satisfaction and engagement.

HBO Max's interface may be considered cluttered or less intuitive compared to other streaming apps, with some users finding it challenging to discover content or navigate between different sections of the app.
